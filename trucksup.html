TrucksUp

Branding, Comunication Illustrations

TrucksUp reached out to Acumen for a campaign that could help them build trust with India's trucking community—particularly with drivers, fleet owners, brokers, and SME businesses. Our first endeavour was to give them a brand strategy to develop an identity that would resonate with their audience. Inspiration came from the daily lives of the truckers themselves—the pocket where they store their earnings.

The tagline 'Ab Apni Chalao' (tr. 'Now Move Your Way') borrowed from Hindi vernacular to create an empowering message. From there, the launch campaign celebrated the drivers' deep connection with their trucks by creating personalised slogans and painting them onto their vehicles. Through interviews and social media content, the campaign reached over 100k people.

Acumen worked on:
Brand strategy | Brand identity | Brand voice | Social media | Tagline | Campaign executions
