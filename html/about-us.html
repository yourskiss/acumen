Acumen is an independent creative studio helping brands come into their own - through simple and effective storytelling.

At Acumen, we catch the pulse of movement between what's worked before and what will work in the future to create an unmatched experience for brands. We're more into in-depth insights and strategic decision-making than getting by with trends.

SHWETA MALHOTRA

A Leica scholar- Shweta is the founder and CEO of Acumen Creative Studio, a self-taught artist and a vivid collector of moments, minds and marketing tactics.
Based out of New Delhi, Shweta holds a wide experience in strategic corporate communications with a creative side-eye hooked on visual art. Her expertise is sought after by prestigious IIT institutes, where she serves as a jury for photography including mentoring aspiring artists.
