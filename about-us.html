Acumen is an independent creative studio helping brands come into their own - through simple and effective storytelling.

At Acumen, we catch the pulse of movement between what's worked before and what will work in the future to create an unmatched experience for brands. We're more into in-depth insights and strategic decision-making than getting by with trends.

The woman behind the work.

Shweta Malhotra — Founder/CEO, Acumen

Self-taught artist and selfie-avoiding photographer Shweta founded Acumen in 2010 as a one-stop shop for clients who were looking for design that wasn't hollow and strategy that wasn't passionless.

Based out of New Delhi, Shweta holds a wide experience in strategic corporate communications—with one eye firmly on the visual arts. In her off-hours, Shweta can be seen on the campuses of India's most prestigious STEM institutes, better known as the IITs. There, she mentors aspiring photographers and sits on the jury at various exhibitions.

Beyond the studio walls, Shweta contributes to several organizations and individuals working to protect underprivileged children. A staunch opponent of micro-management, she empowers her teams to do their very best every day. Her vast experience and disciplined-yet-easygoing approach is the key force behind every Acumen project.
