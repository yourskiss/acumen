Selected Work

TrucksUp

TrucksUp reached out to Acumen for a campaign that could help them build trust with India's trucking community—particularly with drivers, fleet owners, brokers, and SME businesses. Our first endeavour was to give them a brand strategy to develop an identity that would resonate with their audience. Inspiration came from the daily lives of the truckers themselves—the pocket where they store their earnings.

Pair

Pair is a healthcare solutions brand for a modern and medically-aware India. With Acumen, they sought to create a brand identity that would convey their quality and trustworthiness without being too clinical about it.

Gangour

Gangour is one of the most beloved Indian restaurants in Dubai, known for its sweets and QSR experience. Having achieved success with their meld of Indian heritage and contemporary sensibilities, they now wanted that very ethos reflected in their brand, too.

HSBC Golf League

In a country where every bank talks to everyone, HSBC wanted to create a lasting and meaningful relationship with the country's affluent segment.

YU Foods

YU Foods do packaged food differently. Their new-age packaged fare offers culinary staples that are fully free of preservatives and MSGs. YU reached out to Acumen for help with their packaging efforts.
